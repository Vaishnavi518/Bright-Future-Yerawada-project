About Bright Future India

Bright Future India is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth through education, training, and career guidance. We work hand-in-hand with corporate partners to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity.

Our Founder

Kishor Palve

Kishor Palve, born in 1984 in the small village of Udarmal in Maharashtra, grew up with limited role models and a community where most children followed predictable paths. After moving to Mumbai, he too chose his education stream by simply following others. His perspective changed in college when an inspiring Economics professor showed him that work could be meaningful. Motivated to try something new, he briefly ran a vada pav stall, but it was shut down due to lack of a license. This setback strengthened his resolve. A chance meeting with a social worker introduced him to social work, and despite not getting into a reputed institute, he pursued an MSW in Satara. Kishor discovered his passion for guiding adolescents who lacked clarity about their futures. He and his friends began mentoring students and school dropouts, eventually leading to the creation of New Resolution India and its flagship initiative, Bright Future.
